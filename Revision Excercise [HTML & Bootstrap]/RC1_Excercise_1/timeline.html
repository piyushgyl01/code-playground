Key Events in the life of Bill Gates

  • 1955: Bill Gates is born on October 28 in Seattle, Washington.
  • 1975: Co-founded Microsoft with Paul Allen.
  • 1980: Microsoft signs a deal with IBM to provide an operating system (MS-DOS) for their first personal computer.
  • 1985: Launched Windows 1.0.
  • 1995: Windows 95 is released, introducing the Start menu and a new era for personal computing.
  • 2000: Established the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
  • 2008: Transitioned from full-time work at Microsoft to focus on philanthropy.
  • 2010: Gates, along with Warren Buffett, launches the Giving Pledge, encouraging billionaires to commit the majority of their wealth to philanthropy.
  • 2014: Gates steps down as Chairman of Microsoft, continuing to serve as a technology advisor.
  • 2021: Bill and Melinda Gates announced their divorce.
  • Present: Bill Gates continues to be actively involved in philanthropy and addresses global challenges through various initiatives.
