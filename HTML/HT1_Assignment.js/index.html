Biography

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is an Indian former international cricketer who captained the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket.

Achievements

Cricket Records

Sachin Tendulkar hold numerous cricket records, including being the highest run scorer in both ODIs and test cricket. He achieved several milestones throughout his career.

International Honors

He received several records and Honors, including Bharat Ratna, Indian Highest Civilian Award, in 2014. His contributions in Indian Cricket is unparalleled.

Records

Sachin Tendulkar holds records for the most runs in international cricket, most centuries in test and ODIs formats and many more. His records are testament to his exceptional skill and longevity in the sport.
