Biography

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist, and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule.

Philosophy

Ahinsa (Non-violence)

Mahatma Gandhi believed in the power of non-violence as means to bring about social and political change. He practiced and preached the principle of "Ahinsha" which advocates non-violent resistance even in the face of oppression.

Satyagraha (Truth Force)

Gandhis's concept of "Satyagraha" emphasised the pursuit of truth and justice through non-violent means. Ut became a cornerstone for his struggle for India's independence.
